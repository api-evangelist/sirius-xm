<p>From May 4-10, the exclusive SiriusXM channel will air nightly shows, live stand-up and backstage access from the annual event as it happens. Check out the programming schedule below, and tune in live on satellite channel 93 and the <a href="https://sxm.app.link/BlogTagPage-NetflixIsAJokeRadio">SiriusXM app</a> (with some content available on demand on the app after it airs).</p>
<h3><strong>Netflix Is a Joke Fest: How to Listen on SiriusXM</strong></h3>
<h4><strong><a href="https://www.siriusxm.com/blog/tag/what-a-joke-with-papa-fortune">What A Joke with Papa &amp; Fortune</a> with special co-host Eric André</strong></h4>
<p><strong>Live:</strong> Tuesday–Saturday<br />
10pm–12am PT / 1am–3am ET<br />
<strong>Replays:</strong> 10am ET &amp; 5pm ET</p>
<p>Tom Papa and Fortune Feimster team up with Eric André to break down each day of the festival—sharing stories, interviewing performers, and reacting to what just happened on stage.</p>
<p>Miss it live? Episodes will be available on demand under the “What a Joke” section.</p>
<h4><strong>Are You Still Listening? — LIVE from the Fest</strong></h4>
<p><strong>Live:</strong> Wednesday–Friday<br />
7:30–8:30pm PT / 10:30–11:30pm ET</p>
<p>For three nights only, SiriusXM’s exclusive stand-up series goes fully live from the festival.</p>
<p>These shows will not be available on demand, so catching them live is essential.</p>
<h4><strong>Netflix Is a Joke Stand-Up Poker Tournament</strong></h4>
<p><strong>Live:</strong> Tuesday<br />
1–4pm PT / 4–7pm ET</p>
<p>Comedians, cards, and microphones. This live poker tournament brings together comics competing at the table while being interviewed in real time.</p>
<p>This event will be available on demand after it airs.</p>
<h4><strong>“Tight Five” + Backstage Interviews (All Week Long)</strong></h4>
<p>Beyond the live shows, Netflix Is a Joke Radio is rolling out a steady stream of exclusive festival content:</p>
<ul>
<li>“Tight Five,” a fast-paced interview format where comics answer five quick questions</li>
<li>Backstage interviews recorded just before performers hit the stage</li>
</ul>
<p>These segments will air throughout the week with no fixed schedule, giving you constant access to the voices shaping the festival.</p>
<p>They’ll also be available on demand in a dedicated miscellaneous collection.</p>
