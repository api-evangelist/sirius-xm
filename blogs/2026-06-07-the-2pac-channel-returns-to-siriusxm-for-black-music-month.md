The 2Pac Channel is returning to SiriusXM for Black Music Month, available on the app from June 3 through July 3 and on satellite channel 106 from June 15 through June 22. The channel features iconic hits, deep album cuts, live recordings, rare collaborations, and exclusive stories celebrating Tupac Shakur's impact on hip-hop culture. Additionally, LL COOL J's Rock The Bells Radio will host 2Pac Tuesdays every Tuesday in June to honor the artist during Black Music Month.
