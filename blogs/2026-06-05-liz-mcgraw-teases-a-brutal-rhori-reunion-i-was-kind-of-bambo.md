Liz McGraw, a cast member of The Real Housewives of Rhode Island, discussed her experience at the reunion on SiriusXM's Reality Checked program. She characterized the event as "brutal" and stated she felt "kind of bamboozled" during portions of it, explaining that the reunion allowed her to address conflicts from the season. The RHORI reunion Part 1 premieres June 21 on Bravo, and the interview aired on Reality Checked with Dorinda Medley and Kiki Monique on Radio Andy (Ch. 102).
