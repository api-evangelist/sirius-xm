<h3><strong>Adam Scott Has Read All of &#8216;Severance&#8217; Season 3</strong></h3>
<p>When asked how much of the upcoming season he’s seen, Adam didn’t hesitate.</p>
<p>“Oh, yeah. I’ve read the whole – essentially all of it,” he said, noting that his role as a producer gives him deeper access behind the scenes.<img alt="Photo of Adam Scott at SiriusXM." class="aligncenter wp-image-236230" height="563" src="https://blog-admin.siriusxm.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/AdamScott_sarahgoodell_-4032.jpeg?w=300" width="750" /></p>
<h3><strong>He “Possibly” Knows How the Series Ends</strong></h3>
<p>The conversation turned to how long “Severance” might run and whether there’s a defined ending.</p>
<p>While Adam didn’t confirm specifics, he playfully hinted that he may already know where the story is headed.</p>
<p>“We don’t [know how many seasons], but I do,” he said. When pressed further on whether he knows the endpoint, he added, “Sure. Possibly.”</p>

<h3><strong>He says his New Horror Movie Is “So Scary”</strong></h3>
<p>Outside of “Severance,” Adam is heading into horror territory with his upcoming film “Hokum,” opening May 1.</p>
<p>According to Adam, the movie’s intense even for him.</p>
<p>“It is so scary,” he said. “There were screams in the audience… someone peed their pants in the movie theater.”</p>
