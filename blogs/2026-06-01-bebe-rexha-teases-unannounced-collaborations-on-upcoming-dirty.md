Bebe Rexha visited SiriusXM's Miami studios on the Spring Break Sound Check special to discuss her fourth studio album, Dirty Blonde, arriving June 12, 2026. During the interview with hosts Mack and Jen on SiriusXM Hits 1, she teased unreleased collaborations beyond the already-announced tracks New Religion with Faithless and Sad Girls with David Guetta. Rexha confirmed there are additional cool collabs coming that have not yet been announced publicly.
